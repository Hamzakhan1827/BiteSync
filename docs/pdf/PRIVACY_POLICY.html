Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CraveSync is built on the principle that your personal food memories are yours alone. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected.

Who We Are

CraveSync is a food intelligence platform that helps diners track their meals and helps restaurants understand customer feedback. We are based in Karachi, Pakistan.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@cravesync.app

What We Collect

When you create an account

  • Your email address (used for login only)
  • The display name you choose
  • The avatar you select

When you use the app

  • Your reviews: the dish you rated, your thumbs up/down, your public note, and any photo you upload
  • Your private notes (stored encrypted — see below)
  • The dishes you mark as favourites (liked items)
  • Your recent search history (stored locally on your device only — never sent to our servers)

Automatically

  • The date and time of reviews
  • Basic usage data needed to keep the app running (error logs, authentication tokens)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your location
  • Your phone contacts
  • Your phone number (email-only authentication)
  • Any data from other apps on your device
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking

Your Private Notes — Strictly Yours

Private notes you write in the app are your personal food diary. They are:

  • Never shared with restaurants under any circumstances
  • Never included in analytics or aggregate reports
  • Never read by CraveSync staff in normal operations
  • Stored in our database with access controlled at the database level — only your account can retrieve your private notes
This is enforced at the database security level, not just the application level. Even if the app itself were compromised, private notes remain inaccessible to other parties.

Public Reviews

When you choose to write a public note or add a photo to a review, you are making that content visible to:

  • Other CraveSync users browsing that dish
  • The restaurant that prepared the dish (via their dashboard)
Your display name will appear alongside your public reviews. You can delete any public review at any time from your Profile tab.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share individual user data with restaurants. Restaurants see:

  • Aggregate popularity scores (e.g. "76% positive")
  • Public notes and photos you chose to share
  • No names associated with thumbs up/down ratings without a public note
We may share anonymised, aggregated data (e.g. "Nihari is the most-reviewed dish in Karachi this month") for business and marketing purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on Supabase (managed PostgreSQL), hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
  • All connections are encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Access to your data is controlled by Row Level Security — your account can only access your own data
  • Review photos are stored in a secure cloud storage bucket

How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete a review, it is removed immediately. If you delete your account, all your personal data — including private notes, liked items, and reviews — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your reviews and diary entries are visible in the Profile tab
  • Delete any individual review at any time from the Profile tab
  • Delete your account — contact support@cravesync.app and we will process this within 7 business days
  • Correct your display name — update it in your Profile settings

Children

CraveSync is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify users via the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
